Hi, I am
Linfeng Liu

Ph.D. candidate at Tufts in machine learning

Linfeng is a Ph.D. candidate at Tufts University advised by Prof. Liping Liu. His recent research focuses on designing machine learning techniques to advance the analysis of relational data, such as graphs. Linfeng will be joining Meta as research scientist in 2022 summer.

His previous research leverages probabilistic models and graph neural networks to benefit applications in various fields, such as spatial data modeling, biochemical data analysis, and efficient combinatorial problem sovler.

Research Highlights

Efficient Graph Matching using Machine Learning

Align two graphs by defining matchings distribution that is parameterized by graph neural networks. Applications span biochemistry and computer vision.

Accurate Spatial Data Modeling

Flexible graph neural networks that can make use of known labels as input. Combine advantages of graph neural networks and kriging. Image from NOAA.

  • Paper

Amortized Gaussian Process Inference

Accurate and fast GP inference emphasizes strong correlations among data. Graph neural networks significantly speed up inference.
